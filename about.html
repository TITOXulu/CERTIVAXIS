About Certivaxis

Building Trust Through Compliance and Operational Excellence.

Our Story

At Certivaxis, we believe that compliance should be a strategic asset, not a bureaucratic burden. Founded on the principle that "real world" experience is the best teacher, we provide South African businesses with consulting that actually works on the shop floor or in the laboratory.

With over a decade of hands-on industry experience across fuels, chemicals, mining, laboratories, and manufacturing, our founders saw a gap in the market: businesses were struggling with consultants who understood the paperwork but not the processes.

Certivaxis was created to bridge that gap. We bring a deep understanding of ISO standards coupled with practical operations savvy to help you pass certification faster and with less internal chaos.
