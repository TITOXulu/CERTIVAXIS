Precision Compliance for Medical Tech

ISO 13485:2016 is an internationally agreed standard that sets out the requirements for a quality management system specific to the medical devices industry. It is designed to be used by organizations throughout the life cycle of a medical device, from initial design to final decommissioning.

Compliance with ISO 13485 is often a regulatory requirement for medical device manufacturers looking to enter the South African and international markets.

Critical Success Factors:

  • Risk Management: Integrating ISO 14971 principles into your QMS.
  • Design Control: Maintaining detailed design history files (DHF) and device master records (DMR).
  • Sterility Assurance: Implementing controls for sterile device production and packaging.
  • Post-Market Surveillance: Establishing systems for complaint handling and reporting.
