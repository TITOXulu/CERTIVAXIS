Accreditation for competence, impartiality, and consistency.

Elevating Inspection Standards

ISO/IEC 17020:2012 specifies requirements for the competence of bodies performing inspection and for the impartiality and consistency of their inspection activities. It applies to inspection bodies of type A, B or C, as defined in ISO/IEC 17020:2012, and it applies to any stage of inspection.

In South Africa, SANAS accreditation to ISO 17020 is a powerful mark of quality that opens doors to government contracts and large-scale private sector projects.

Key Requirements for Success:

  • Impartiality & Independence: Establishing the necessary safeguards to ensure inspection integrity.
  • Personnel Competence: Formalizing the qualifications, training, and experience requirements for inspectors.
  • Inspection Methods: Standardizing procedures and equipment management.
  • Reporting: Ensuring inspection reports and certificates meet international standards.
