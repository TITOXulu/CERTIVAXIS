ISO/IEC 17025:2017 Laboratory Accreditation

Ensuring technical competence and valid results for testing and calibration laboratories.

Excellence in Lab Operations

ISO/IEC 17025:2017 is the international standard for testing and calibration laboratories. It enables laboratories to demonstrate that they operate competently and generate valid results, thereby promoting confidence in their work both nationally and around the world.

Accreditation to this standard is essential for laboratories that want their results to be globally accepted by regulatory bodies and industrial partners.

Our Technical Focus:

  • Method Validation: Ensuring your testing methods are fit for purpose and scientifically sound.
  • Measurement Uncertainty: Calculating and reporting the reliability of your test results.
  • Equipment Calibration: Establishing traceability to national and international standards.
  • 90-Day Pass Guarantee: A specialized laboratory roadmap to get you cert-ready in 90 days, guaranteed.
  • Proficiency Testing: Coordinating and analyzing PT results to demonstrate ongoing competence.
